Frequently Asked Questions

Is there Linux / MacOS support?

Currently there's no plan to support Linux / MacOS operative systems, however, we encourage developers to fork our source code and make ports to other operative systems.

Why doesn't any beatmap show up?

The "songs" folder may have not been autodetected. Go to Settings [üî©], click on "'Songs' Directory" and set the "Songs" folder of your Osu! installation.
If the problem persists, it may be a bug, you can submit it on my Discord server.
